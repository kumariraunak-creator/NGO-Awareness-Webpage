About InAmigos Foundation

Empowering Lives Through Education, Humanity & Sustainable Development

We Believe Small Steps Can Change The World.

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to improving lives through education, women empowerment, environmental conservation, food distribution, animal welfare and youth engagement.

Our volunteers work across different communities to spread awareness, encourage social responsibility, and create sustainable impact.

Our Ongoing Projects

Creating positive change through our community initiatives.

Project Seva

We organize food distribution drives, donate clothes, provide essential supplies and support underprivileged families with compassion.

Project Prakriti

Tree plantation campaigns and environmental awareness programs to make India greener and healthier.

Project Bachpanshala

Free education, digital learning and personality development for children from underprivileged communities.

Project Udaan

Empowering women through awareness, education, skill development and leadership opportunities.

Project Jeev

Caring for stray animals by providing food, rescue support and medical care while promoting animal welfare.
